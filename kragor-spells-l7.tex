  range=120 ft,
  components={V, S},
  duration=Instantaneous,

  attack=Ranged,

]{Eldritch Blast}

\textbf{Cantrip Upgrade.} The spell creates two beams at level 5, three beams at level 11, and four beams at level 17. You can direct the beams at the same target or at different ones. Make a separate attack roll for each beam.

\begin{Spell}[
  level=Cantrip,
  time=1 Action,
  range=30 ft,
  components={V, S},
  duration=1 Minute,
  school=Conjuration,
  attack=None,
  effect=Utility
]{Mage Hand}
A spectral, floating hand appears at a point you choose within range. The hand lasts for the duration. The hand vanishes if it is ever more than 30 feet away from you or if you cast this spell again.

When you cast the spell, you can use the hand to manipulate an object, open an unlocked door or container, stow or retrieve an item from an open container, or pour the contents out of a vial.

As a Magic action on your later turns, you can control the hand thus again. As part of that action, you can move the hand up to 30 feet.

The hand can't attack, activate magic items, or carry more than 10 pounds.

\begin{components}
V & \textit{Veni et iuva me (WEH-nee eht YOO-wah meh)}
\end{components}
\end{Spell}

    level=Cantrip,

    range=30 ft/5 ft cube,
    components={S, M},
    duration=1 Minute,
    school=Illusion,
    attack=None,
    effect=Control
]{Minor Illusion}
You create a sound or an image of an object within range that lasts for the duration. See the descriptions below for the effects of each. The illusion ends if you cast this spell again.

If a creature takes a Study action to examine the sound or image, the creature can determine that it is an illusion with a successful Intelligence (Investigation) check against your spell save DC. If a creature discerns the illusion for what it is, the illusion becomes faint to the creature.

\textbf{Sound.} If you create a sound, its volume can range from a whisper to a scream. It can be your voice, someone else's voice, a lion's roar, a beating of drums, or any other sound you choose. The sound continues unabated throughout the duration, or you can make discrete sounds at different times before the spell ends.

\begin{Spell}[
    level=1st,
    time=1 Action,
    range=60 ft,
    components={V},
    duration=Instantaneous,
    school=Enchantment,
    attack=WIS,
    effect=Prone
]{Command}
You speak a one-word command to a creature you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or follow the command on its next turn. Choose the command from these options:

\textbf{Approach.} The target moves toward you by the shortest and most direct route, ending its turn if it moves within 5 feet of you.

\textbf{Drop.} The target drops whatever it is holding and then ends its turn.

\textbf{Flee.} The target spends its turn moving away from you by the fastest available means.

\textbf{Grovel.} The target has the Prone condition and then ends its turn.

\textbf{Halt.} On its turn, the target doesn't move and takes no action or Bonus Action.

V & \textit{C\=og\=eris (koh-GAY-riss)}
\end{components}
\end{Spell}

  tags=Concentration,
  level={2nd},
  time={1 action},
  range={Self},
  components={V, S, M},
  duration={1 minute},
  school={Divination},
  attack={WIS Save},
  effect={Detection}
]{Detect Thoughts}
You activate one of the effects below. Until the spell ends, you can activate either effect as a Magic action on your later turns.

\textbf{Sense Thoughts.} You sense the presence of thoughts within 30 feet of yourself that belong to creatures that know languages or are telepathic. You don't read the thoughts, but you know that a thinking creature is present.

The spell is blocked by 1 foot of stone, dirt, or wood; 1 inch of metal; or a thin sheet of lead.

\textbf{Read Thoughts.} Target one creature you can see within 30 feet of yourself or one creature within 30 feet of yourself that you detected with the Sense Thoughts option. You learn what is most on the target's mind right now. If the target doesn't know any languages and isn't telepathic, you learn nothing.

Either way, the target knows that you are probing into its mind, and until you shift your attention away from the target's mind, the target can take an action on its turn to make an Intelligence (Arcana) check against your spell save DC, ending the spell on a success.
