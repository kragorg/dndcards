\begin{Spell}[
  tags=Concentration,
  level={2nd},
  time={1 Action},
  range={60 ft/10 ft cube},
  components={V, S, M},
  duration={1 minute},
  school={Illusion},
  attack={INT Save},
  effect={Psychic}
]{Phantasmal Force}
You craft an illusion in the mind of a creature you can see within range. The target must make an Intelligence saving throw. On a failed save, you create a phantasmal object, creature, or other phenomenon of your choice (no larger than a 10-foot cube) that is perceivable only to the target for the duration. The phantasm includes sound, temperature, and other stimuli.

The target treats the phantasm as if it were real, rationalizing any illogical outcomes. If the phantasm is a dangerous creature or hazard, it deals 2d8 Psychic damage to the target on each of your turns if the target is in the phantasm's area or within 5 feet of it. The target perceives the damage as a type appropriate to the illusion.

The target can take a Study action to make an Intelligence (Investigation) check against your spell save DC. On a success, the target realizes the phantasm is an illusion, and the spell ends.

\begin{components}
V & \textit{Phantasma mortiferum (fan-TAZ-mah mor-TIF-er-um)} \\
M & a bit of fleece
\end{components}
\end{Spell}
