\begin{Spell}[
  level=Cantrip,
  time=1 Action,
  range=10 ft,
  components={V, S},
  duration=1 Hour,
  school=Transmutation,
  attack=None,
  effect=Utility
]{Prestidigitation}
You create a magical effect within range. Choose the effect from the options below. If you cast this spell multiple times, you can have up to three of its non-instantaneous effects active at a time.

\textbf{Sensory Effect.} You create an instantaneous, harmless sensory effect, such as a shower of sparks, a puff of wind, faint musical notes, or an odd odor. \textbf{Fire Play.} You instantaneously light or snuff out a candle, a torch, or a small campfire. \textbf{Clean or Soil.} You instantaneously clean or soil an object no larger than 1 cubic foot. \textbf{Minor Sensation.} You chill, warm, or flavor up to 1 cubic foot of nonliving material for 1 hour. \textbf{Magic Mark.} You make a color, a small mark, or a symbol appear on an object or a surface for 1 hour. \textbf{Minor Creation.} You create a nonmagical trinket or an illusory image that can fit in your hand. It lasts until the end of your next turn. A trinket can deal no damage and has no monetary worth.

\begin{components}
V & \textit{Ludo magica (LOO-doh MAH-gee-kah)}
\end{components}
\end{Spell}
