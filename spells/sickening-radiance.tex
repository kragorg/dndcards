\begin{Spell}[
  tags=Concentration,
  level={4th},
  time={1 action},
  range={120 ft/30 ft r sphere},
  components={V, S},
  duration={10 minutes},
  school={Evocation},
  attack={CON Save},
  effect={Radiant, Exhaustion}
]{Sickening Radiance}
Dim, greenish light fills a 30-foot-radius sphere centered on a point you choose within range. The light spreads around corners and lasts for the duration.

When a creature moves into the spell's area for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there, it must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take 4d10 radiant damage, suffer one level of exhaustion, and emit dim, greenish light in a 5-foot radius. This light prevents the creature from benefiting from being invisible.

The light and any levels of exhaustion caused by this spell go away when the spell ends.

\begin{components}
V & \textit{Lux aegra (LOOKS EYE-grah)}
\end{components}
\end{Spell}
