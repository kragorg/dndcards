\begin{Spell}[
  tags=Concentration,
  level={3rd},
  time={1 action},
  range={120 ft/30 ft cube},
  components={S, M},
  duration={1 minute},
  school={Illusion},
  attack={WIS Save},
  effect={Control}
]{Hypnotic Pattern}
You create a twisting pattern of colors in a 30-foot cube within range. Each creature in the area who sees the pattern must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, the creature has the Charmed condition for the duration. While Charmed this way, the creature is Incapacitated and has a speed of 0.

The spell ends for a creature if it takes damage or if another creature uses its action to shake it out of its stupor.

\begin{components}
M & a pinch of confetti
\end{components}
\end{Spell}
