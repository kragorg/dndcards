\begin{Spell}[
  tags=Concentration,
  level={3rd},
  time={1 action},
  range={150 ft/20 ft r sphere},
  components={V, S, M},
  duration={1 minute},
  school={Conjuration},
  attack={DEX Save},
  effect={Damage, Control}
]{Hunger of Hadar}
You open a gateway to the Far Realm. A 20-foot-radius Sphere of Darkness appears, centered on a point you can see within range, and it lasts for the duration. The Sphere is Difficult Terrain.

No light, magical or otherwise, can illuminate the area, and creatures fully within it are Blinded. Whispers and slurping noises can be heard up to 30 feet away.

A creature that starts its turn in the area takes 2d6 Cold damage. A creature that ends its turn there must make a Dexterity saving throw or take 2d6 Acid damage.

\textbf{Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.} The Cold or Acid damage (your choice) increases by 1d6 for each spell slot level above 3.

\begin{components}
V & \textit{Vorax nihilo (WOR-ax NEE-hee-lo)} \\
M & a pickled tentacle
\end{components}
\end{Spell}
