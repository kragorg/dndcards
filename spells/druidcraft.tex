\begin{Spell}[
  level=Cantrip,
  time=1 Action,
  range=30 ft,
  components={V, S},
  duration=Instantaneous,
  school=Transmutation,
  attack=None,
  effect=Utility
]{Druidcraft}
Whispering to the spirits of nature, you create one of the following effects within range.

\textbf{Weather Sensor.} You create a Tiny, harmless sensory effect that predicts what the weather will be at your location for the next 24 hours. The effect might manifest as a golden orb for clear skies, a cloud for rain, falling snowflakes for snow, and so on. This effect persists for 1 round.

\textbf{Bloom.} You instantly make a flower blossom, a seed pod open, or a leaf bud bloom.

\textbf{Sensory Effect.} You create a harmless sensory effect, such as falling leaves, spectral dancing fairies, a gentle breeze, the sound of an animal, or the faint odor of skunk. The effect must fit in a 5-foot Cube.

\textbf{Fire Play.} You light or snuff out a candle, a torch, or a campfire.

\begin{components}
V & \textit{Natura susurro (nah-TOO-rah soo-SOOR-roh)}
\end{components}
\end{Spell}
