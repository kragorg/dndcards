\begin{Spell}[
  tags=Concentration,
  level={3rd},
  time={1 action},
  range={90 ft},
  components={V, S, M},
  duration={1 hour},
  school={Conjuration},
  attack={None},
  effect={Summoning}
]{Summon Fey}
You call forth a Fey spirit. It manifests in an unoccupied space you can see within range and uses the Fey Spirit stat block. When you cast the spell, choose a mood: Fuming, Mirthful, or Tricksy. The creature resembles a Fey creature of your choice marked by the chosen mood, which determines certain details in its stat block. The creature disappears when it drops to 0 Hit Points or when the spell ends.

The creature is an ally to you and your companions. In combat, the creature shares your initiative count, but it takes its turn immediately after yours. It obeys your verbal commands (no action required by you). If you don't issue any commands, it takes the Dodge action and uses its movement to avoid danger.

\textbf{Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.} Use the spell slot's level for the spell's level in the stat block.

\begin{components}
V & \textit{Voco fae (WOH-koh FEYE)} \\
M & a gilded flower worth at least 300 gp
\end{components}
\end{Spell}
