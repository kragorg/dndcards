\begin{Spell}[
  tags=Concentration,
  level={1st},
  time={1 action},
  range={30 ft},
  components={V, S, M},
  duration={1 minute},
  school={Enchantment},
  attack={WIS Save},
  effect={Control}
]{Tasha's Hideous Laughter}
A creature you can see within range must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, it falls Prone, becomes Incapacitated, and laughs uncontrollably for the duration. The creature can't end the Prone condition on itself.

At the end of each of its turns, and each time it takes damage, the target makes another Wisdom saving throw, ending the spell on a success. The save has Advantage if triggered by damage.

\textbf{Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.} You can target one additional creature for each spell slot level above 1.

\begin{components}
V & \textit{Risus demens (REE-soos DAY-mens)} \\
M & a tart and a feather
\end{components}
\end{Spell}
