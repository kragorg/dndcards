\begin{Spell}[
    level=1st,
    time=1 Action,
    range=60 ft,
    components={V},
    duration=Instantaneous,
    school=Enchantment,
    attack=WIS,
    effect=Prone
]{Command}
You speak a one-word command to a creature you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or follow the command on its next turn. Choose the command from these options:

\textbf{Approach.} The target moves toward you by the shortest and most direct route, ending its turn if it moves within 5 feet of you.

\textbf{Drop.} The target drops whatever it is holding and then ends its turn.

\textbf{Flee.} The target spends its turn moving away from you by the fastest available means.

\textbf{Grovel.} The target has the Prone condition and then ends its turn.

\textbf{Halt.} On its turn, the target doesn't move and takes no action or Bonus Action.

\begin{components}
V & \textit{C\=og\=eris (koh-GAY-riss)}
\end{components}
\end{Spell}
