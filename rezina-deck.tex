  \par \ThinRule

  \textit{Musical Instrument}, \textit{Simple Melee Weapon}

  This shallow drum is reinforced with an iron rim and a sturdy oak cross-brace
  on the back, allowing it to be gripped like a shield. Bards often use the
  pommel of a sword or a rhythmic ``tipper'' stick to play it while keeping their
  other hand ready for combat.

  \textbf{Special Property (Reverberating Block):} While you are holding this
  weapon, you can use your Reaction when you are hit by a ranged weapon attack
  to angle the drum against the flight path. Make a Charisma (Performance)
  check against the attack roll that hit you.

  \begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Success:} If your check is higher than the attack roll, the
    projectile deflects off the drum's rim or taut skin. You reduce the
    damage taken by your Charisma modifier.
    \item \textbf{Failure:} You take the damage as normal.
  \end{itemize}
